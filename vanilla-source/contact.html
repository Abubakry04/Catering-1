Frequently Asked

Common Questions

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance for large events (200+ guests) and 2–3 weeks for smaller gatherings. For peak seasons (December, Easter), we advise booking 3+ months ahead to secure your date.

Do you offer tasting sessions?

+

Yes! We offer complimentary tasting sessions for bookings of 100+ guests. For smaller events, we offer a tasting package for ₦15,000 (redeemable against your final invoice). Contact us to schedule yours.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

+

Absolutely. We cater to halal, vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, dairy-free, and nut-free requirements. Simply indicate your guests' dietary needs during the booking process and we'll tailor the menu accordingly.

What's your minimum guest count?

+

Our minimum is 20 guests for standard catering packages. For intimate private dining experiences, we do offer a fine dining popup service with a minimum of 6 guests — contact us for pricing.

Do you serve outside Lagos?

+

Yes! We regularly serve events in Abuja, Port Harcourt, Ibadan, and Enugu. Travel and logistics surcharges apply. We've also served diaspora events in Ghana and UK. Contact us to discuss your location.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept bank transfers, Paystack, and cash payments. A 40% deposit is required to confirm your booking, with the balance due 7 days before the event. Payment plans can be arranged for large bookings.
