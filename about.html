Who We Are

Built on Lagos Roots,
World-Class Standards

LuxeCater began in a modest kitchen in Surulere, Lagos, with our founder Chef Emeka Okonkwo and a single wedding booking. Twelve years later, we've served over 500 events across six states and earned a reputation as Nigeria's most trusted premium caterer.

Our philosophy is simple: use the freshest ingredients, respect traditional recipes, and execute with the precision of a Michelin-starred kitchen. We blend authentic Nigerian flavours with modern technique — creating menus that feel both familiar and extraordinary.

From intimate 20-person dinner parties to grand 2,000-guest wedding receptions, we bring the same uncompromising standards, meticulous attention to detail, and genuine warmth to every event we touch.
